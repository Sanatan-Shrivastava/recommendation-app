About us

Our prime objective is to discuss the ongoing research and development towards detecting dysgraphia, by using the given handwriting of the person that is being tested on by using a set of Machine Learning/Deep Learning Algorithms and other generic python libraries. Another objective is to lay out a foundation on the types of Dysgraphia and their causes. To devise some qualitative methods of identifying the disorders with the help of ML/DL methods, it is possible to understand how a person with dysgraphia is affected in a learning environment and in a workplace environment.
